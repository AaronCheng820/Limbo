6 # Introduction {#Parsers_LefParser_Introduction}
7 
8 [LEF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Library_Exchange_Format) together with [DEF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Design_Exchange_Format) is a common file format in VLSI physical design.
9 Generally LEF/DEF formats are quit complicated due to the complex cases in chip design.
10 This parser is adjusted from the open source LEF parser released by [Cadence Design Systems](https://www.cadence.com) with C++ wrappers.
11 The original parsers lie in the [Thirdparty package](@ref ThirdParty).
12 Users have to follow the [LICENSE](@ref Parsers_LefParser_License) agreement from the original release.
13 It is tested under various academic benchmarks for VLSI placement.
